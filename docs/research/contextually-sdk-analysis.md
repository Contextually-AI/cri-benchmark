### 5. Ideas to Carry Forward

- What code or data could be reused or adapted?
- What naming or terminology should be preserved?

### 6. Differences from CRI
- How does CRI's design improve on the prototype?
- What new capabilities does CRI add?
- What has been deliberately changed?
